Project Overview

Tank Battle is a browser-based top-down shooter where you control a tank using WASD to move and the mouse to aim and fire. Enemy tanks spawn from the edges of the map and hunt you down ‚Äî you need to destroy them before they destroy you.

As kills stack up, enemies grow faster and tougher, and a second type of enemy ‚Äî the Sniper ‚Äî begins to appear. Snipers keep their distance, leading shots to predict your movement. Killing enemies earns money which can be spent on upgrades between waves.

Key Features

  • Two Enemy Types: Standard tanks that strafe and close in, and Snipers that hold range and lead shots based on your velocity.
  • Upgrade Shop: Spend earned money on movement speed, turret speed, bullet speed, fire rate, max health, and bullet damage ‚Äî all upgradeable across multiple tiers.
  • Escalating Difficulty: Enemy speed and health scale with kill count. Double spawns begin after kill 7, and the spawn interval tightens over time.
  • Particle Effects: Explosion particles on kills, impact sparks on bullet hits, muzzle flash on every shot, and a red vignette when the player takes damage.
  • Crate Drops: Heal and money crates spawn periodically across the map to give you a fighting chance.
  • Spawn Indicators: Edge arrows warn you where the next enemy will arrive, pulsing faster as the spawn timer runs down.
  • Track Marks: All tanks leave fading tread marks on the ground as they move.
